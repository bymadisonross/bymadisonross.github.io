Education

I hold a BSBA in Information Systems from the University of Florida with a minor in Economics. My success is deeply rooted in my faith, driving me to seek out opportunities to develop strong technical, interpersonal, and leadership skills while investing in others.

The Warrington College of Business at the University of Florida provides
a platform for students to develop personally and professionally through
engagement in student-run organizations and by utilizing Warrington’s
network of students, alumni, and staff. Warrington has propelled me
forward in my college career by providing mentors that challenge me to
create an impactful and lasting legacy.

Their investment has inspired me to explore my passions, establish
relationships with staff and fellow students, and give back to the
community. My most significant and lasting contributions are identifying
opportunities for students to explore technology-focused careers in
Warrington and gain relevant experience to excel in their career trajectory.

Developing Cultural Intelligence Abroad, my Reflections on Dublin, Ireland

What I did not expect, however, was a difference in way of life,
from the laissez-faire times stores opened and closed to the
ambiguity in how assignments were communicated and accomplished.

Throughout my past month exploring Ireland and adapting to their
work culture, I have gained the knowledge to recognize how different
groups try to understand each other, and how I can respond using
my cultural intelligence to break through the lens of a “single story”
and establish myself as a unique individual and valued coworker
in an interconnected world.
