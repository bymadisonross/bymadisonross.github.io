Hi, I'm Madison! I'm currently a Technology Consultant at Protiviti, Speaker for Women Who Code's Data Science Track, and Co-Director of Protiviti's Global Diversity and Inclusion Book Club.

I'm responsible for helping businesses manage their technology more effectively and securely while taking a data-driven approach to solving their toughest challenges. My work is guided by a strong belief in the power of experimentation and behavioral analysis to revolutionize strategic initiatives.

I'm passionate about streamlining processes utilizing advanced analytics and machine learning techniques, discovering and reducing bias in algorithms, and humanizing technology for the accessibility of society's most vulnerable.

My mission is to cultivate an inclusive workplace culture by widening individual perspectives and establishing a support system to empower more minorities to pursue careers and leadership roles in technology.
